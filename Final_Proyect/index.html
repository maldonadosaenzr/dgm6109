about us

did you know that a betta fish can live up to five years? and a goldfish till fifteen! so many fishes are not taking care of and abused everyday just because
"having a fish bowl is enough"
which is wrong! as any animal they need proper care and love,our mission is to help new pet owners
(specially the "owners by surprise") and starting aquarist to learn about the world of freshwater fishes,
having to take care of a new lil guy can be scary, but with us it can be lots less scary!
so for those that really want to have the empathy to learn and care for our little companions; welcome!
